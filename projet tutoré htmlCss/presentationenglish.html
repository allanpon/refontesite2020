The computer science departments of France's IUTs train students in computer science and teamwork in order to integrate them into professional life.

They thus offer training on a human scale, supervised by available teachers, as well as by experienced professionals who provide students with practical experience. and concrete of the IT professions.

Teaching in computer science (DUT or Licence Professionnelle) is based on 3 axis, each of them requiring specific qualities and skills:

Analysis and design:

Preliminary work in relation with the customer to specify his needs and expectations and the design of a computer processing scheme.

Communication, open-mindedness, capacity for analysis and synthesis ...

Algorithms and programming :

Effective implementation of processing up to coding.

Logical spirit, technical precision ...

System and network architecture :

Operation of a computer, its operating system and networks (Internet, Intranet, ...).

Curiosity, patience ...

In addition to these targeted courses, half of the general education disciplines are added (Mathematics, Economics and Management, English and Communication, etc.). This gives the training a multidisciplinary aspect.

In addition, the evaluation is not only based on theory but also on real-life situations, via tutored projects and an internship in a company, enabling the knowledge and skills acquired during the training to be validated.
