Testimony of Damien Lefebvre

With his A-levels in his pocket, Damien Lefebvre spent a few months in college before reorienting himself in search of a more supervised education. His university experience having revealed a pronounced taste for computer science, he decided to apply to the IUT of Villetaneuse to prepare a DUT in Computer Science with a Software Engineering option in 2002..

The supervision offered by passionate teachers and the acquisition of rigorous work methods encouraged him to complete his training via the Licence Professionnelle Systèmes Informatique et Logiciel option Génie Logiciel, offered by the same IUT.

"The knowledge and skills developed through three years of studying algorithms, databases, but also the accounting aspects of the business world, were the key that enabled me to be recruited by a major French IT services company, after a 4-month internship at the end of the year".

For a year and a half now, the young Programmer Analyst has been putting his skills at the service of major clients, including renowned banking institutions, and wishes to focus on "Consulting" in the future.
