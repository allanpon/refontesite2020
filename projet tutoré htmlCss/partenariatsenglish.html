Training partner companies :

The IT departments of the IUTs have as partners large IT companies. This allows students to integrate these companies more easily at the end of their training, via the end-of-year internship.

Here is a non-exhaustive list of partner companies:

  • Atos Origin
  • Bull
  • Capgemini
  • Cigref
  • IBM
  • Microsoft
  • Syntec
  • Unilog

International partnership :

In addition to professional partnerships, IT departments in partnership with foreign universities welcome foreign students to train them in computer science. But also by sending students to do their internship abroad.

L' Brasilia universities is a foreign partner university.
