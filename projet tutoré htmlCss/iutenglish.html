University institutes of technology

The University Institutes of Technology in France are spread throughout the country and number 115, which ensures a strong anchoring in the local and regional economic environment. Meetings between department directors of the IUTs ensure the national coherence of education.

The IUT has a quality of university education recognized and plebiscited by companies for more than 40 years, thanks to the implementation of programs adapted to the professional reality and the creation of diplomas responding to the emergence of new professions.
